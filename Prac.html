PhysX 4.0

The NVIDIA PhysX SDK is a scalable multi-platform physics solution supporting a wide range of devices, from smartphones to high-end multicore CPUs and GPUs. The PhysX SDK is now open source,available under a BSD 3 license.



Nsight Graphics 2019.1
